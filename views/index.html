Case Studies

An Illinois based Manufacturing Company manufacturing Cables, Wires and Connectors wanted an Assistant Manager Purchase and Assistant Manager Assembly. The Purchase Manager had to have high level of team management skills to handle a team of 30 members. The Assembly Manager had to be skilled in moving the technology from automation to Manpower. Our team successfully closed this position which was open for some time

A Canadian based MNC in IT Services required two LEAD ANALYSTS with Skills in Terradata, Linux, Solaris, and Network Administration. The Challenge was to find these candidates who could join at short notice. Although there are more developers with Terradata skillset it was hard to find Administrators in Terradata. Our recruiters worked using their entire network and found the right candidates and successfully on-boarded both the candidates. Since then our team is a preferred vendor for all key positions of this client.

A Global Software Product development services company which is into core engineering and IT Consulting services wanted a Lead developer having niche skill with the Informatica in Cloud skillset and another Lead Developer having skills like Java with SQL database(Like Mongo Db,Couch DB,Cassandra) in Banking / Digital e-commerce domains. The challenge was finding a technical Lead who was an individual contributor and to get him to join at short notice as the project was already on. Our recruiters took on the challenge and sourced the best candidate and were successfully On-boarded within 15 days. The client appreciated us for closing the position with a quick TAT and within the Salary Slab.

From one of the globally established organization, we received a requirement to fill Technical Writer role. They were looking for a candidate who is sited within 10 miles from their branch office location so that he/she can work from Remote location and be capable of draw closer to office on a need basis. Client was in search of candidate for few months with the support of their various Pre-Approved vendors. But they were not able to fill the position due to client's low budget on this specific requirement and the remote location. We took this up as a real confront and deployed a dedicated Delivery team to work on this requirement and our head hunters responded very well by making a submission of best qualified candidate's resume after scrutinizing various profiles. We have filled that position within 48 hours and the candidate has joined within a short period of time.
